# Facebook Posting Compliance Checklist

This checklist is the allowed place to document prohibited wording examples. Normal post copy and CSV queue content must not use prohibited wording. Content must preserve Paper Only, 不構成投資建議, and ENABLE_LIVE_TRADING=false.

## 1. Allowed Wording

- Paper Trading
- Paper Only
- 策略研究
- 回測
- 資料治理
- 風控流程
- OMS
- 交易基礎設施
- 不構成投資建議
- Browser-only Interactive Demo
- ENABLE_LIVE_TRADING=false
- 本社群不喊單、不代操、不提供投資建議
- 內容僅供研究、教育與系統開發討論

## 2. Prohibited Wording

The following examples are prohibited in normal post copy:

- 保證獲利
- 穩賺
- 零風險
- 自動賺錢
- 跟單獲利
- 代操
- 本金保證
- 不會虧損

English equivalents such as profit guarantees, safety-minimizing claims, managed-account claims, signal-selling claims, or copy-trading claims are also prohibited in normal post copy.

## 3. Investment Advice Boundary

Allowed:

- Explain platform workflow.
- Explain data, rollover, backtest, Paper Trading, Risk Engine, OMS, audit evidence.
- Invite users to try the browser-only Web App demo.

Not allowed:

- Individualized entry/exit suggestions.
- Position sizing recommendations for a real account.
- Claims that a strategy should be followed.
- Claims that simulated PnL is investment performance.

## 4. Futures Advisory / Managed Account Boundary

Do not present the platform as a futures advisory service, managed account operator, broker, or discretionary trading service. If a comment asks for personal account operation, decline and redirect to education.

## 5. Copy Trading / Signal Service Boundary

The platform does not sell signals, copy trading, or instructions to follow a strategy. Strategy content must remain research-only and signals-only.

## 6. Facebook Spam And Repetitive Content Boundary

- Do not mass-post identical content.
- Do not ask for fake reactions, fake comments, or fake sharing.
- Do not use manipulative engagement wording.
- Do not post in unrelated groups.
- Vary hook, body, CTA, and visual by channel.

## 7. Third-Party Group Posting Rules

Before posting in a related group:

1. Read the rules manually.
2. Confirm educational posts are allowed.
3. Confirm links are allowed.
4. Adapt the post for that group.
5. Do not post if promotional content is prohibited.

## 8. Post Approval Checklist

- [ ] One CTA only.
- [ ] Clear Paper Only or education framing.
- [ ] Includes 不構成投資建議 when product or demo is mentioned.
- [ ] Does not claim production trading readiness.
- [ ] Does not mention broker credentials or account data collection.
- [ ] Does not include prohibited wording outside this checklist.

## 9. Comment Moderation Checklist

Remove or decline:

- personal trade requests
- broker credential requests
- real account screenshots with personal data
- claims of future results
- unsafe broker/live trading expectations

Safe response:

```text
本社群只討論研究、教育與系統開發流程，不提供投資建議、喊單、帳戶操作或實盤委託協助。你可以先使用 Web App 的 Paper Only / browser-only Demo 了解平台流程。
```

## 10. Takedown Checklist

Take down or edit content if:

- wording can be interpreted as investment advice
- a group rule was missed
- a screenshot includes private data
- a comment thread shifts into unsafe account operation requests
- the post creates confusion about live trading status

Live trading remains disabled by default.
